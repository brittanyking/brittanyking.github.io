Junior year of highschool I went on an EF tour to Costa Rica with myself, a group of students, and a teacher. There we learned about their culture and did many hands on activities such as horseback riding and zip lining through the jungle, hiking up active volcanoes, boating through the rivers, and much more. It was a once in a life time experience that I will cherish forever!
